The Citizens' Track and [PAIRS](https://pairs.site) teams will be atin Geneva from 6th - 10th July, for the UN Global Dialogue on AI Governance, AI for Good, and WSIS events. 

## All week - 7th - 10th July in the AI for Good Expo

Stop by our booth to see demos of the case book, chat with the team and find out more about practical approaches to participatory AI governance across the world. 

## Tuesday 7th July - Graduate Institute Geneva - Breakfast Roundtable (08:45 - 10:00) 

Join us for the launch of the Citizens’ Track on AI report and the Case Book and sharing visions for the future of public voice shaping AI governance. 

In-person and online participation options.

## Wednesday 8th July - Citizens’ Track on AI Governance: From Theory to Practice at UN HQ (16:15 - 18:00)

As part of a day of Multistakeholder Convening on AI Governance organised in partnership with PAIRS, Geneva Graduate Institute, MAP-AI, Global Network Initiative, Global South Network on Trustworthy AI, OHCHR B-Tech Project, Business for Social Responsibility (BSR), this session will bring together practitioners, funders, and advocates focused on inclusive and democratic AI governance to co-design practical next steps that can deliver a Citizens’ Track on AI governance linked to both the ongoing UNGDAI and the 2027 Geneva AI Summit.

## PAIRS Breakfast - 9th July

Join the Participatory AI Research and Practice Symposium team for an informal breakfast meet-up, ahead of a PAIRS Stewards meeting planning for PAIRS 2027. Details tbc.
